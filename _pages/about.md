I am a medical physicist at the **Proton Therapy Center, Samsung Medical Center** in Seoul, Korea. My work focuses on **proton beam therapy** — particularly Monte Carlo simulation, patient-specific quality assurance, range verification, and treatment planning for line-scanning and pencil-beam-scanning systems.

## Research Interests

- Proton therapy physics (Monte Carlo, FLUKA, TOPAS, Geant4)
- Patient-specific QA for pencil beam scanning & line scanning
- In-vivo range verification (prompt gamma, gamma electron vertex imaging)
- Secondary neutron dose assessment
- Dosimetry for re-irradiation, HDR brachytherapy, and tomotherapy
- Deep-learning approaches for dose distribution analysis

## Background

Before transitioning to medical physics, I worked in theoretical high-energy physics — holographic QCD, AdS/CFT, and strongly-coupled plasma hydrodynamics (JHEP, Physical Review D). Earlier publications appear at the bottom of the publications list.
